Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 10th July, 2025

Welcome to Quantum Vibes (“we”, “our”, “us”). Your privacy is our top priority. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal data when you use the Quantum Vibes mobile app (“the App”), which uses AI and your device camera to estimate wellness metrics like heart rate — without storing or sharing your images or results.

1. What We Collect

We collect the following information to offer you personalized health insights:

a. Basic Personal Details

  • Name
  • Email address (for login or support)
  • Age or date of birth
  • Gender
  • Height and weight

b. Camera Access & Biometric Estimates

  • The App uses your camera for real-time facial scanning to estimate heart rate and related vitals.
  • We do not store, save, or transmit any images, videos, or raw scan data.
  • All processing happens locally on your device.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use your data solely to:

  • Provide real-time feedback on your health metrics
  • Personalize your wellness insights and progress tracking
  • Improve the accuracy and functionality of the App

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or third-party marketing.

3. No Image or Result Storage

  • Images or face scan data are never saved, stored, or sent to any server — not even temporarily.
  • Health estimates or results (like heart rate) are shown only to you within the app session and are not stored unless you explicitly choose to track them locally.
  • We do not use cloud services to analyze or keep any biometric or personal data.

4. No Data Sharing

  • We do not sell, share, or disclose your data to any third party.
  • We may share non-personal, anonymized statistics (e.g. feature usage trends) to improve app performance — but not your personal or health information.

5. Permissions

  • Camera – required to perform real-time facial scanning. You can disable this at any time via device settings.
  • Optional permissions – like notifications or device storage (only if you choose to save health logs).

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access or update your personal data
  • Revoke permissions at any time (e.g., camera access)
  • Request deletion of all local data (we store nothing on our servers)

7. Security Measures

Although we don’t store sensitive data, we use best practices to protect any information you choose to save in the app — including device-level encryption and secure local storage.

8. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in technology or regulations. We will notify you of major updates within the app.
